About SahiFab

Our leadership team has been working in the digital printing solutions for over 10 years. We are doing constant research efforts for the past 5 years. Now, we are launching, made in India hemp fibers, yarns and fabrics from licensed fields in India.

We owe our success to our hardworking team, our customers, and investors who provide high-quality products and services. We are committed to saving the planet by minimizing detrimental impact on the environment through the use of sustainable products. Our goal is to help our customers realize their vision for sustainable textile solutions. We welcome you to reach out to us and find out how we can assist you in achieving your sustainable goals.
